Cosplay Tips

Disclaimer, these tips are from personal experiences and others experiences, cosplay doesn't really have any rules to follow (besides basic human decency), most stuff here is just opion based and tips from a cosplayer that's been doing cosplay for about 7 years.

Why People do Cosplay

Cosplay is a huge subgenre of Weeb Culture and gets people into fandoms and spaces of less judgment, it also improves skills that a lot of people use in day to day life including: hair styling, makeup skills, crafting and building, sewing, and many other skills. People love cosplaying for multiple different reasons; the feeling of being a character, wig styling and experimenting with different techniques, doing different makeup that is uncommon for everyday, crafting and painting props, making friends with people that have simlar interests, learning new things from people, cosplay contests, and so many others that I can't list because the list is endless. Everyone starts off somewhere in cosplay and advance at certain levels. Cosplay is more like an endless learning experiment rather than a hobby with strict rules. People get to express themselves and show nerdy interest that others judge harshly but people do it anyway, because it builds confidence and the feeling of happiness overrules all judgment.

There's multiple different techniques of cosplay tips, including;

  • Airbrushing
  • Crimping and steaming wigs
  • Using EVA foam for props and armor
  • Using liquid latex for fake wounds
  • Embroidering tiny details
  • 3d printing
